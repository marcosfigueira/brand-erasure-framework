# FAQ

## What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

GEO is the process of optimizing brands, content and digital infrastructure for visibility inside AI-generated answers.

## What is Agentic Optimization (AgO)?

Agentic Optimization refers to the optimization of systems, APIs and infrastructures for autonomous AI agents capable of executing tasks.

## What is Brand Erasure?

Brand Erasure is the condition in which AI systems satisfy a user need without surfacing, citing or transacting with the originating brand.

## What is delegated consumer-AI agency?

Delegated consumer-AI agency describes situations where consumers delegate part of their decision-making process to AI systems.

## What is the difference between SEO, AEO, GEO and AgO?

SEO focuses on search rankings.
AEO focuses on extractable answers.
GEO focuses on synthesized AI visibility.
AgO focuses on agentic task execution.

## Why does semantic visibility matter?

AI systems increasingly mediate discovery, recommendation and transaction decisions.

## Why is structured data important for AI systems?

Structured data reduces verification cost and increases machine interpretability.
