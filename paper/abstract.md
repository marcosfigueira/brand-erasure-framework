# Abstract

The competitive ground of digital visibility has shifted from webpages to AI-generated answers and increasingly toward autonomous actions performed by AI agents.

This paper proposes a unified framework integrating Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), and Agentic Optimization (AgO) under delegated consumer–AI agency.

The article introduces Brand Erasure as a strategic risk in AI-mediated markets and develops propositions linking semantic visibility, interoperability, information gain and attribution persistence to brand outcomes inside AI systems.
